Our Projects



ResCUer App

Team members: Yiwei Ni, Matthew Barker, Naxin Chen, Raymone Radi

Brought back from an existing app, ResCUer aims to increase campus safety by providing emergency services information, blue-light information and more at your finger tips.

TCAT Web App

Team members: Ashton Cooper, Paviya (Elle) Tojaroon, Horace He

The web tool aims to make it easier for the average Cornell student to hop on a bus and get to their next location by creating an intuitive ride planning app with the TCAT bus schedule.



Course Reviews

Team members: Divyansha Sehgal, Matthew Barker, Vinisha Mittal, Jesse Mansoor, Daniela Gottesman

A website comprised of all the courses at Cornell as well as student submitted course review of each course, difficulty level, course load, expected class sizes and more. It aims to helps students learn more about the courses they are taking before they start taking them.

O-Week App

Team members: Julia Kruk, Juhwan Park, Vicente Caycedo, David Chu, Arnav Ghosh

Working with the Cornell Orientation Steering committee, the team is working on creating mobile app for incoming freshmen to use during Orientation Week. The app displays all scheduled events for the week, the ability to add events to your own schedule, filters for different types events and map directions to get each event!



Project Samwise

Team members: Alice Ran Zhou, Justin Tran, Kevin Li, Kevin Gregory, Irene Yoon

A visualization of a student's semester at Cornell. Students will able to add classes and have information automatically populate into their calendar, suggested dates to start homework/assignments and colors to show their busiest weeks in the semester.

What is

SA Tech?



Cornell SA Tech is an up-and-coming project team affiliated with the Cornell Undergraduate Student Assembly. Founded in Fall 2015, we are a group of students who are passionate about making a change in our community through design and technology. Our team consists of 35 product managers, designers and developers working on over 5 projects ranging from a campus safety app to a course review website. Check out our github to see what we're up to!
